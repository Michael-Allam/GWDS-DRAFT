About US

Meet The Owners: Mary & Jonas

Mary and Jonas both share a passion for books, coffee and art. Together, they sought out to create a space for all book-lovers and creatives to come and unwind from the hustle of the city life.

At MJ's, grab a bite, have some coffee and read a book from our large collection. Out with friends? Choose a boardgame to play from our shelves! And don't forget to join us for a live performance and open mic event every week!

Our Goal

We are more tham just a coffee shop. At MJ's, We aspire to provide an experience, creating an friendly atmosphere where creativity thrives.
We offer a Full Cafe Menu - A Collection of books to read from - A Performance Stage where we hold weekly wevents - Our Board Game Library - And Our Artists Area, where we encourage you to leave your mark.
