We Are a Coffee Lounge & Book Store, Built Upon a Love for Hospitality, Novels and Art

An Experience At MJ's Brings You: Coffee, Books, Live events & Performances, as well as Board Games and Art

Meet The Owners

Mary and Jonas both share a passion for books, coffee and art. Together, they sought out to create a space for all book-lovers and creatives to come, unwind and take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city life.

At MJ's, grab a bite, have some coffee and read a book from our large collection. Out with friends? Choose a boardgame to play from our shelves! And don't forget to join us for live performances and open mic events every week!
